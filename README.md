## The Problem
Mathematics is the language of the universe, but for many, it remains a wall of text. Concepts like **Monodromy**â€”how mathematical objects change as you move around a singularityâ€”are often buried in dry textbooks.

* **Too Abstract:** Hard to visualize in the mind's eye.
* **Too Static:** Formulas on paper don't "move."
* **High Barrier:** Usually reserved for advanced topology students.

## The Solution
I built a reactive, educational engine that turns complex analysis into a neon-soaked puzzle game.

* **Interactive Controls:** Drag squares (coefficients) to drive the dots (roots).
* **Real-time Visualization:** See the "dance" of roots on the complex plane instantly.
* **Gamified Learning:** Guide the roots to specific targets to understand the underlying group theory structure.
